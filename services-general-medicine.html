Services

General Medicine

At SDHRC, we offer a comprehensive range of General Medicine services for adults, including preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment of various medical conditions. Our team of highly trained and experienced general medicine practitioners or internists is dedicated to providing quality care to our patients. SDHRC provide a wide range of general medicine services to help patients manage their health and recover from illness or injury. Our services includes:

  • Consultations: consultations with general medicine physicians who can provide patients with information on how to maintain good health and prevent illness.
  • Diagnosis: diagnostic tests to help identify and diagnose various medical conditions. These tests include blood tests, imaging tests like digital X-rays, and other tests such as electrocardiograms (ECGs).
  • Treatment: treatments for various medical conditions, including medication, physical therapy, and surgery.
  • Chronic Disease Management: we also provide care for patients with chronic diseases such as diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease. They offer ongoing monitoring, medication management, and lifestyle counselling to help patients manage their conditions.
  • Emergency Care: emergency medical services for patients who require immediate care for conditions such as heart attacks, strokes, and injuries.
  • Rehabilitation Services: rehabilitation services to help patients recover from injuries, illnesses, and surgeries.
  • Preventive Care: preventive care services such as vaccinations, health screenings, and health education to help patients maintain good health and prevent the onset of illness.

Overall, we offer a wide range of general medicine services to help patients manage their health and recover from illness or injury.
